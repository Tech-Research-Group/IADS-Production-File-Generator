This can lead to serious injury or death of personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>

			<symptom>No power at TRICON Refrigerated Container System (TRCS).</symptom>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">No power at PDB.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<note>
					<trim.para>This corrective action will determine if fault is local to TRCS or part of a larger issue. If larger issue is found, user will be directed to appropriate troubleshooting procedure.</trim.para>
				</note>
				<step1>
					<para>Identify PDB (<xref figid="T00013-PDB_ID"/>) supplying power to TRCS.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00013-PDB_ID">
					<title>Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem Connections</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800235"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Check for proper operation of Expeditionary TRICON Kitchen System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/>, billeting TEMPER shelter connected to same PDB IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>, and dining TEMPER shelter IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS (any check): Proceed to malfunction "TRCS MAIN CB tripped or OFF."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Troubleshoot symptom "No power to all systems at one power distribution box (PDB)" IAW <xref wpid="T00017-10-5419-218"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">TRCS MAIN CB tripped or OFF.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Observe position of MAIN CB <callout assocfig="T00013-Main_CB" label="1"/> and proceed as follows:</para>
					<figure id="T00013-Main_CB">
						<title>MAIN CB</title>
						<graphic boardno="G06021801176"/>
					</figure>
					<step2>
						<para>ON: Proceed to malfunction "Power cable damaged or disconnected."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>OFF: Set MAIN CB <callout assocfig="T00013-Main_CB" label="1"/> to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>Tripped: Set MAIN CB <callout assocfig="T00013-Main_CB" label="1"/> to OFF position, then back to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/> if CB immediately trips.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check TRCS for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "Power cable damaged or disconnected."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">Power cable damaged or disconnected.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Inspect power cables for damage and proper connection from TRCS to PDB (<xref figid="T00013-PDB_ID"/>). No damage and proper connection should be found.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Proceed to malfunction "PDB CB tripped or OFF."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Damage found): Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Cable disconnected): Proceed to <xref stepstart="T00013-Cable_Disco"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1 id="T00013-Cable_Disco">
					<para>Identify PDB CB (<xref figid="T00013-PDB_CB"/>) supplying power to TRCS.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00013-PDB_CB">
					<title>PDB 60-A CB and Output Receptacles</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800007"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00013-PDB_CB"/>) to OFF position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect power cable <callout assocfig="T00013-Shelter_Recpt100" label="4"/> to TRCS power cable <callout assocfig="T00013-Shelter_Recpt100" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00013-Shelter_Recpt100">
					<title>TRCS Power Connection</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801177"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect cable dust cover <callout assocfig="T00013-Shelter_Recpt100" label="3"/> and TRCS cable dust cover <callout assocfig="T00013-Shelter_Recpt100" label="2"/> together.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00013-PDB_CB"/>) to ON position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check TRCS for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "No power from PDB, TRCS CB failure, or TRCS power cable failure."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
