		<faultproc>
			<title/>
				<warning>
				<icon-set boardno="Electrical_Symbols"/>
				<icon-set boardno="Slick_Floor"/>
				<icon-set boardno="Falling"/>
				<trim.para>Power setup of Expeditionary Platoon Life Support Module (EPLSM) may vary from site to site. Correct circuit breaker (CB) can be identified by following power cables from component back to power distribution box (PDB). Failure to comply may result in power remaining at component. This can lead to serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
				<trim.para>All jewelry must be removed before starting work. Metal objects, such as rings or tools, can cause short circuits when contacting live circuits. A direct short can cause instant heating of metal resulting in severe burns. Failure to comply may result in serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if exposed to electricity.</trim.para>
				<trim.para>Interior and exterior areas of EPLSM may be a wet environment. Electrical cables and controls should not be handled with wet hands or while standing in water. Failure to comply may result in serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
				<trim.para>Cable, hose, and other equipment layout should be accomplished to minimize risk of trip hazards to personnel during operation. Failure to comply may result in injury to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
			</warning>
			<symptom>No power at TEMPER shelter.</symptom>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">No power at PDB.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<note>
					<trim.para>This corrective action will determine if fault is local to TEMPER shelter or part of a larger issue. If larger issue is found, user will be directed to appropriate troubleshooting procedure.</trim.para>
				</note>
				<step1>
					<para>Identify PDB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_ID"/>) supplying power to affected TEMPER shelter.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00020-PDB_ID">
					<title>Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem Connections</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800235"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>TEMPER shelters are connected to all PDBs. Common items will depend on affected TEMPER shelter. Only common items need to be checked for this test.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Check for proper operation of Expeditionary TRICON Kitchen System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/>, any other TEMPER shelters IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>, TRICON Refrigerated Container System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/>, Expeditionary Latrine System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-209-13&amp;P"/>, Expeditionary Shower System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-210-13&amp;P"/>, and/or Expeditionary TRICON Self Serve Laundry System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-215-10"/>.</para>
					</specpara>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS (any check): Proceed to malfunction "Power cable damaged or disconnected."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Troubleshoot symptom "No power to all systems at one power distribution box (PDB)" IAW <xref wpid="T00017-10-5419-218"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">Power cable damaged or disconnected.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Inspect power cables for damage and proper connection from TEMPER shelter to PDB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_ID"/>). No damage and proper connection should be found.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Proceed to malfunction "PDB CB tripped or OFF."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Damage found): Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Cable disconnected): Proceed to <xref stepstart="T00020-Cable_Disco"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1 id="T00020-Cable_Disco">
					<para>Identify PDB CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) supplying power to TEMPER shelter.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00020-PDB_CB">
					<title>PDB 60-A CBs and Output Receptacles</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800031"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) to OFF position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove dust cover <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="2"/> from power cable <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="4"/> and dust cover <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="3"/> from TEMPER shelter distribution box POWER IN receptacle <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00020-PDB_Connection">
					<title>TEMPER Shelter Distribution Box Connection</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801135"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect power cable <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="4"/> to TEMPER shelter distribution box POWER IN receptacle <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect power cable dust cover <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="2"/> and POWER IN receptacle dust cover <callout assocfig="T00020-PDB_Connection" label="3"/> together.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) to ON position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check TEMPER shelter for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "No power from PDB or POWER IN J1 receptacle failure."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">PDB CB tripped or OFF.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Identify PDB CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) supplying power to TEMPER shelter.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Observe position of affected CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) and proceed as follows:</para>
					<step2>
						<para>ON: Proceed to malfunction "No power from PDB or POWER IN J1 receptacle failure."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>OFF: Set affected CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>Tripped: Set affected CB (<xref figid="T00020-PDB_CB"/>) to OFF position, then back to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC if CB immediately trips.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check TEMPER shelter for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-224-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "No power from PDB or POWER IN J1 receptacle failure."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">No power from PDB or POWER IN J1 receptacle failure.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<para>Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC.</para>
			</action>
