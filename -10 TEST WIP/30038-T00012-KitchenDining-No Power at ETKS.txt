			<symptom>No power at Expeditionary TRICON Kitchen System (ETKS).</symptom>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">No power at PDB.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<note>
					<trim.para>This corrective action will determine if fault is local to ETKS or part of a larger issue. If larger issue is found, user will be directed to appropriate troubleshooting procedure.</trim.para>
				</note>
				<step1>
					<para>Identify PDB (<xref figid="T00012-PDB_ID"/>) supplying power to ETKS.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00012-PDB_ID">
					<title>Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem Connections</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800235"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Check for proper operation of billeting or dining TEMPER shelters IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/> and TRICON Refrigerated Container System IAW <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/> connected to same PDB.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS (any check): Proceed to malfunction "ETKS CB 1/3/5 tripped or OFF."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Troubleshoot symptom "No power to all systems at one PDB" IAW <xref wpid="T00017-10-5419-218"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">ETKS CB 1/3/5 tripped or OFF.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Rotate latch <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="2"/> to open ETKS power distribution panel assembly <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="3"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00012-Main_CB">
					<title>MAIN CB</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801093"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>ETKS has two MAIN CBs labeled CB 1/3/5. Troubleshooting procedure should be performed on each CB.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Observe position of CB 1/3/5 <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="1"/> and proceed as follows:</para>
					</specpara>
					<step2>
						<para>ON: Proceed as follows:</para>
						<step3>
							<para>Close and secure ETKS power distribution panel assembly <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="3"/> by rotating latch <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="2"/>.</para>
						</step3>
						<step3>
							<para>Proceed to malfunction "Power cable damaged or disconnected."</para>
						</step3>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>OFF: Set CB 1/3/5 <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="1"/> to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>Tripped: Set CB 1/3/5 <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="1"/> to OFF position, then back to ON position.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by ETKS MAC IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/> if CB immediately trips.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Close and secure ETKS power distribution panel assembly by rotating latch <callout assocfig="T00012-Main_CB" label="2"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check ETKS for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "Power cable damaged or disconnected."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
			<malfunc label="malfunction">Power cable damaged or disconnected.</malfunc>
			<action>
				<step1>
					<para>Inspect power cables for damage and proper connection from ETKS to PDB (<xref figid="T00012-PDB_ID"/>). No damage and proper connection should be found.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Proceed to malfunction "PDB CB tripped or OFF."</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Damage found): Send to next level of maintenance as authorized by MAC.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL (Cable disconnected): Proceed to <xref stepstart="T00012-Cable_Disco"/>.</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
				<step1 id="T00012-Cable_Disco">
					<para>Identify PDB CB (<xref figid="T00012-PDB_CB"/>) supplying power to ETKS.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00012-PDB_CB">
					<title>PDB 100-A CB and Output Receptacles</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800602"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00012-PDB_CB"/>) to OFF position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove dust covers <callout assocfig="T00012-Power_Cable" label="2"/> from power cables and receptacle <callout assocfig="T00012-Power_Cable" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="T00012-Power_Cable">
					<title>100-A Power Cable Assembly</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801336"/>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect power cable to receptacle <callout assocfig="T00012-Power_Cable" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Connect power cable and receptacle dust covers <callout assocfig="T00012-Power_Cable" label="2"/> together.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Set applicable PDB CB (<xref figid="T00012-PDB_CB"/>) to ON position.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check ETKS for proper operation IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/>.</para>
					<step2>
						<para>PASS: Return to normal operation.</para>
					</step2>
					<step2>
						<para>FAIL: Proceed to malfunction "No power from PDB, ETKS CB failure, or POWER IN receptacle failure."</para>
					</step2>
				</step1>
			</action>
