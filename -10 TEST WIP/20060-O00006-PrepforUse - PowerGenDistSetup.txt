							<trim.para>Some Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem components are heavy. Fuel tank weighs 62 lb (28.5 kg) empty and is a two-person lift. Power distribution box (PDB) weighs 185 lb (83 kg) and is a six-person lift. Fuel Distribution System (FDS) weighs 375 lb (170 kg) and must be moved by four personnel or material handling equipment (MHE). Generator weighs 3,600 lb (1,650 kg) and requires MHE to move. Follow safety precautions when moving heavy items. Personnel must not stand between MHE and item or below item while it is being moved. Serious injury or death to personnel may result in safety precautions are not observed. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Hearing can be permanently damaged if personnel are exposed to constant high noise levels of 85 dB or greater. Wear approved hearing protection devices when operating or working within 61 ft (19 m) of MHE when engine is running. Personnel exposed to high noise levels shall participate in a hearing conservation program in accordance with <extref docno="DA PAM 40-501"/>. Hearing loss occurs gradually but becomes permanent over time. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
						</warning>
