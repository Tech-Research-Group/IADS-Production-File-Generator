				<title>Set Up ESS</title>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<warning>
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-hand"/>
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-wall"/>
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-above"/>
							<icon-set boardno="Ear_Protection_Symbol"/>
							<trim.para>ESS weighs 6,350 lb (2,880 kg) and must be moved using MHE or other suitable lifting device. Personnel must not stand between MHE and ESS or below ESS while it is being moved. Failure to comply may result in serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Hearing can be permanently damaged if personnel are exposed to constant high noise levels of 85 dB or greater. Approved hearing protection devices must be worn when operating or working within 61 ft (19 m) of MHE when engine is running. Personnel exposed to high noise levels shall participate in a hearing conservation program in accordance with <extref docno="DA PAM 40-501"/>. Hearing loss occurs gradually but becomes permanent over time.</trim.para>
						</warning>
						<note>
							<trim.para>ESS is placed with power and water connections to outside. ESS front (with data plate) faces site reference point, with ESS left side (power and water side) toward long edge of camp.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Place ESS at site of marking flag (<xref figid="O00017-10-5419-218-F0002"/>), with front right corner of ESS touching marking flag.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<figure id="O00017-10-5419-218-F0002">
					<title>Position ESS</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800064"/>
				</figure>
				<step1 id="O00017-10-5419-218-S0008">
					<para>Perform DEPLOY ESS WING SECTIONS, UNPACK ESS, and SET UP ESS EXTERIOR IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-210-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<caution>
							<trim.para>ESS vents need space to open freely. Ensure area around vent is clear of debris or equipment. Blocking vents may negatively impact ESS operation.</trim.para>
						</caution>
						<para>Perform SET UP ESS INTERIOR IAW <extref docno="TM 10-5419-210-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>

					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Water heater must be placed within 10 ft (3.0 m) of ESS source water pump and within 25 ft (7.6 m) of ESS hot water connection.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Measure 6.5 ft (2 m) from reference corner on shelter along long edge of Expeditionary Platoon Life Support Module (EPLSM) toward site reference point, and 8 ft (2.4 m) along short edge of camp toward site reference point. Mark this location with a flag. This will be deployment location of water heater. Refer to <xref figid="O00017-10-5419-218-F0004"/>.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>

							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Object"/>
							<trim.para>Water heater weighs over 490 lb (223 kg) and must be moved using MHE or other suitable lifting device. Personnel must not stand between MHE and generator or below generator while it is being moved. Failure to comply may result in serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
						</warning>
						<para>Using MHE, place water heater centered at flag placed in <xref stepstart="O00017-10-5419-218-S0015"/> and position water inlet facing site reference point.</para>
