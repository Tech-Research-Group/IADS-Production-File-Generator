		<eqpdesc>
			<para0>
				<title>Characteristics</title>
				<para>The Expeditionary Platoon Life Support Module (EPLSM) (<xref figid="G00002-10-5419-218-F0001"/>) is a complex of containerized Expeditionary TRICON System units (kitchen, laundry, shower, and latrine); generators; Tent, Extendable, Modular, Personnel (TEMPER) shelters; and support equipment.</para>
				<para>
					<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0001">
						<title>Deployed EPLSM</title>
						<graphic boardno="G06021800468"/>
					</figure>
				</para>
				<para>The EPLSM is capable of providing field feeding, hygiene, and sheltering for 75 personnel. Each 75-person EPLSM complex consists of eight different subsystems:
					<randlist bullet="yes">
						<item>Latrine Subsystem - One Expeditionary Latrine System (ELS).</item>
						<item>Laundry Subsystem - One Expeditionary TRICON Self Serve Laundry System (ETSSLS).</item>
						<item>Shower Subsystem - One Expeditionary Shower System (ESS) and one water heater.</item>
						<item>Kitchen/Dining Subsystem - One Expeditionary TRICON Kitchen System (ETKS), one TRICON Refrigerated Container System (TRCS), and one Type-37 TEMPER shelter.</item>
						<item>Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem - Three generators, three power distribution boxes (PDBs), two 1,000-gal (3,785-L) fuel tanks with spill containment systems, and one Fuel Distribution System (FDS).</item>
						<item>Billeting Subsystem - Four Type-31 TEMPER shelters.</item>
						<item>Administration Subsystem - One Type-31 TEMPER shelter.</item>
						<item>Transportation/Storage Subsystem - Nine TRICONs used for EPLSM equipment shipment and storage.</item>
					</randlist>
				</para>
				<para>Energy efficiency components include solar shades (<xref figid="G00002-Solar_Shades"/>), TEMPER shelter rigid doors, and insulated tent liners.</para>
				<para>
					<figure id="G00002-Solar_Shades">
						<title>Deployed EPLSM with Solar Shades</title>
						<graphic boardno="G06021800016"/>
					</figure>
				</para>
			</para0>
			<?Pub _newpage?>
			<para0>
				<title>Capabilities and Features</title>
				<para>The EPLSM (<xref figid="G00002-10-5419-218-F0003"/>) provides the following field sustainment capabilities and features:
					<randlist bullet="yes">
						<item>ETKS provides ovens, griddle, steam kettle, under-counter refrigerator, and a 3-well sanitation center. A TRCS is provided to support the ETKS, and a Type-37 TEMPER shelter provides a dining facility.</item>
						<item>Four climate-controlled Type-31 TEMPER shelters capable of housing 75 personnel.</item>
						<item>One climate-controlled Type-31 TEMPER shelter provides administration area and sleeping quarters for administration personnel.</item>
						<item>ETSSLS provides four commercial washers and dryers.</item>
						<item>One ESS provides four shower stalls and four sinks with mirrors. An external, fuel-fired water heater delivers 9 GPM (34.1 LPM) of hot water at 60-190 &deg;F (16-88 &deg;C).</item>
						<item>One ELS provides four commercial toilets, two urinals, and two sinks with mirrors.</item>
						<item>Three generators and PDBs provide 7-day unattended power generation for the EPLSM complex.</item>
						<item>FDS with a 1,000-gal (3,785-L) fuel tank and fuel nozzle stand provides a retail fuel station in the EPLSM complex.</item>
						<item>EPLSM systems have independent water storage and waste collection.</item>
					</randlist>
					<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0003">
						<title>EPLSM Major Components</title>
						<graphic boardno="G06021800479"/>
					</figure>
				</para>
			</para0>

	<locdesc>
		<title>Location and Description of Major Components</title>
		<para/>
		<comp-item>
			<title>LATRINE SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The ELS provides personal hygiene facilities. Components of ELS can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-5419-209-13&amp;P"/>. The Latrine Subsystem (<xref figid="G00002-10-5419-218-F0004"/>) provides additional power cables to feed ELS from Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem. EPLSM also provides garden hoses with nozzles, brooms, dustpans, and other cleaning supplies to support latrine facilities.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0004">
					<title>Latrine Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801284"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>LAUNDRY SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>One ETSSLS is provided to supply a self serve laundry facility. Components of ETSSLS can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-5419-215-10"/>. The Laundry Subsystem (<xref figid="G00002-10-5419-218-F0005"/>) provides additional power cables to feed ETSSLS from the Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem. EPLSM also provides trash can, broom, and dustpan as cleaning supplies.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0005">
					<title>Laundry Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801285"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>SHOWER SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The Shower Subsystem consists of one ESS <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0006" label="2"/> and a water heater <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0006" label="1"/> which together provide personal hygiene showers and sinks at field locations.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0006">
					<title>Shower Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800478"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
			<para>Components of ESS can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-5419-210-13&amp;P"/> and components of the water heater can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-4520-266-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
			<para>The Shower Subsystem also supplies additional power cables to feed ESS from Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem. The water heater draws power from the waste pump of ESS and is supplied fuel from a drum provided by the Shower Subsystem.</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>KITCHEN/DINING SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>ETKS <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="1"/> is complexed to one Type-37 TEMPER shelter <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="3"/> to form a dining facility (DFAC) supported by one TRCS <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="2"/> and one environmental control unit (ECU) <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="4"/>.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007">
					<title>Kitchen/Dining Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021801286"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
			<para>Components of ETKS can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-5419-207-10"/>, components of dining TEMPER shelter can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>, components of TRCS can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-8145-224-13&amp;P"/>, and components of ECU can be found in <extref docno="TM 10-4120-453-13&amp;P"/>.</para>
			<para>SOURCE water bag <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="6"/> and WASTE water bag <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="7"/> are components of ETKS with optional, altered positioning from stand-alone ETKS usage. Additional hose is provided by Kitchen/Dining Subsystem to support altered position. The Kitchen/Dining Subsystem also supplies additional power cables to feed the ETKS, TRCS, and dining TEMPER shelter from the Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem.</para>
			<para>Dining TEMPER shelter is supplied with energy-efficiency components (insulated liners, LED lighting) preinstalled. Rigid doors are installed during setup to further reduce energy consumption and provide easier ingress/egress. Tables and benches are supplied for dining facility along with ice maker <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0007" label="5"/> capable of producing 290 lb (131 kg) of ice per day. Cleaning supplies (trash cans, mops, brooms, etc.) are supplied to support DFAC operations. Floodlight and extension cord are supplied for outdoor lighting as required.</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>POWER GENERATION AND FUEL DISTRIBUTION SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem provides power to all EPLSM subsystems via electrical power cables. The Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem is made up of three 60-kW generators <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="3"/>, three PDBs <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="4"/>, two 1,000-gal (3,786-L) fuel tanks <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="1"/>, spill containment systems <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="2"/> and attached hose fittings.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009">
					<title>Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800916"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
			<para> Also included in the Power Generation and Fuel Distribution Subsystem is the FDS <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="6"/>, fuel hose <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="7"/>, a reducer <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="5"/>, a nozzle stand assembly, and a spill containment system <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0009" label="2"/>. It provides a retail fuel station and all EPLSM fuel services.</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>BILLETING SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The Billeting Subsystem consists of four Type-31 TEMPER shelters <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010" label="1"/> with ECUs <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010" label="2"/>, bunk beds <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010" label="3"/>, lockers <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010" label="4"/>, four chairs <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010" label="5"/> per shelter, and accessory items to provide shelter and comfort for EPLSM personnel. Each billeting TEMPER shelter is equipped with integrated lighting, a billeting TEMPER shelter distribution box that controls lighting, and convenience outlet power for tent occupants. For more information on TEMPER shelters, refer to <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0010">
					<title>Billeting Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800942"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>ADMINISTRATION SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The Administration Subsystem consists of one Type-31 TEMPER shelter <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="1"/> with an ECU <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="6"/>, office furniture <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="2"/>, contingency spares lockers <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="5"/>, bunk beds <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="3"/>, lockers <callout assocfig="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011" label="4"/>, and accessory items to provide shelter and comfort for the EPLSM and administration personnel. Administration TEMPER shelter is equipped with an administration TEMPER shelter distribution box that controls the integrated lighting and convenience outlets for shelter occupants. For more information on TEMPER shelters, refer to <extref docno="TM 10-8340-244-13&amp;P"/>.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0011">
					<title>Administration Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800943"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
		</comp-item>
		<?Pub _newpage?>
		<comp-item>
			<title>TRANSPORTATION/STORAGE SUBSYSTEM COMPONENTS</title>
			<para>The Transportation/Storage Subsystem is composed of nine TRICONs (<xref figid="G00002-10-5419-218-F0012"/>) with interior modifications for EPLSM item transportation and storage. Once the EPLSM is deployed, the TRICONs are used for storage.
				<figure id="G00002-10-5419-218-F0012">
					<title>Transportation/Storage Subsystem Components</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06021800474"/>
				</figure>
			</para>
		</comp-item>
