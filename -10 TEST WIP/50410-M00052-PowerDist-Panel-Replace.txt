	<warning>
		<icon-set boardno="Electrical_Symbols"/>
		<trim.para>Power setup of Expeditionary Platoon Life Support Module (EPLSM) may vary from site to site. Correct circuit breaker (CB) can be identified by following power cables from component back to power distribution box (PDB). Failure to comply may result in power remaining at component or being applied to wrong component. This can lead to serious injury or death of personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if injury occurs.</trim.para>
		<trim.para>High voltage is present. Power cables should not be connected or disconnected while power is applied to equipment. All electrical safety precautions must be followed when performing maintenance. Failure to comply may result in death or serious injury to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if exposed to electricity.</trim.para>
		<trim.para>All jewelry must be removed before starting work. Metal objects such as rings or tools can cause short circuits when contacting live circuits. A direct short can cause instant heating of metal resulting in severe burns. Failure to comply may result in serious injury or death to personnel. Seek immediate medical attention if exposed to electricity.</trim.para>
	</warning>
